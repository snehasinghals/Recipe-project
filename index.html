Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Add milk gradually to make a soft dough.
  3. Rest the dough for 10 minutes, then roll into smooth, small balls.
  4. Deep-fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  5. Prepare sugar syrup with sugar, water, and cardamom.
  6. Soak the fried balls in warm syrup for 30 minutes and serve warm.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add condensed milk and milk powder.
  2. Cook the mixture on low heat while stirring continuously.
  3. When it thickens and leaves the sides, transfer to a greased tray.
  4. Flatten and garnish with chopped dry fruits or silver leaf.
  5. Let it cool, then cut into square pieces and enjoy!
